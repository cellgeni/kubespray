Single-cell RNA-Seq map of the human biliary tree

Single cell transcriptomes of 35,603 cells isolated from different regions of the biliary tree (intrahepatic bile ducts, common bile duct, gallbladder) and from organoids propagated from cholangiocytes from each region. Different organoids were treated with gallbladder bile for 72 hours.
Origin annotation Primary tissue (PRI), untreated organoids (ORG), treated organoids (ORGT).
Region annotation Intrahepatic Bile Ducts (IHD), Common Bile Duct (CBD), Gallbladder (GB).
